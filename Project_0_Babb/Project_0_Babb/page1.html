Welcome To Artheria!

Warriors from the four corners of Artheria, gather round! The call has come for legends and you have answered! Now battle will be joined, for only a select number pass through trial by honorable combat can be allowed access in the halls of legend!
  • Classes
  • Fighter: Str ***, Dex *, Con **. The fighters are powerful warriors, armed with great hammers requiring even the stoutest man two hands to use!

  • Paladin: Str **, Dex *, Con ***. Blessed by the gods, the Paladins heal throughout their fights while guarded by mighty shields.

  • Ranger: Str *, Dex ***, Con **. Coming from the densest wood, the rangers are armed with two shortswords, slashing wildly at their opponents as they move swiftly around the battlefield.
